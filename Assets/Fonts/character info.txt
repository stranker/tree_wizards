THIS IS AN ORIGINAL FONT.
It simulates, to me, hand-wrought iron with maybe 50 to 100 years on it... about 200 years ago.

CHARACTER SET:

This font includes all the usual standard keyboard characters. I made it to fill a gap for a "common folk" sort of
pseudo-colonial old-timey font set. The characters kern pretty well and words are spaced a little more than most.
It almost looks like home-made movable type on a home-made press from a "black sheep" descendant of the
Gutenberg clan... and that's what I like about it.
